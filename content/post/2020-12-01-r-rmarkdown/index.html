The following names represent the same country:

United States = USA Russia = Russian Federation Congo (Brazzaville) = Congo Congo (Kinshasa) = DR Congo Trinidad and Tobago = Trinidad & Tobago Somaliland region = Somalia Bosnia and Herzegovina = Bosnia-Herzegovina Ivory Coast = Cote d’Ivoire
